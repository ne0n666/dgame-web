What is the GAME??

The GAME is a social drinking game. It's simple, quick and it gets the party started FAST! So get your buddies together and try it out!

OK, everyone's here. What now?

It's simple:

  1. First, everyone gets a drink.
  2. The GAME is played in turns. When it's your turn, click the square in the middle.
  3. You will see you task on the screen.
  4. There are different types of tasks - some will make you drink, some will make others drink and some will be games that everyone plays together.
  5. Almost all tasks will result in someone taking a sip of their drink.

And that's it! You play until you get bored (or rather until you pass out).

Got it, any other tips?

  • Make sure everyone gets refills as needed.
  • Be fair - drink when you should and don't force others to drink more than they feel like.
  • Remember, this is a game so HAVE FUN!
